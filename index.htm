Dakshana Foundation

Dakshana Foundation - Infinite good
I am also a alum of DAKSHANA FOUNDATION.
It is basically a NGO that helps bright and impoverished students predominantly from rural India to prepare for IIT JEE and medical entrance exams for free of cost.
I was also enrolled there (Dakshana Bangalore Centre) in Two year programme for my IIT JEE preparation. Dakshana operates from Irvine, California and Pune in India.
Those two years were the best part of my life as I had some of the best days of my life. It's been almost 5 to 6 years I left Dakshana foundation but I feel like day before yesterday only I came out of this heaven.
For more info. visit https://dakshana.org/

JNV.

Navodaya - A Home away from your home.
Jawahar Navodaya Vidhyalayas(JNV) are residential schools from class 6th to 12th all over the country, where students do not just study, they play together ,eat together ,make friends for life and grow together. I studied in JNV Jamui,Bihar from class 6th to 10th only as I got selected in Dakshana Foundation in class 11th. But I was the part of JNV Bangalore Urban in class 11th and 12th.
For a Navodayan JNV isn't just a school but a lot more than that. We cry the day we enter in the campus full of strangers and surely we cry the day we leave after 12th(+2). And after that for me,it is not a schools it is a feeling, a bundle of emotions,and a family with whom I lived for six years.
For more info. visit https://navodaya.gov.in
